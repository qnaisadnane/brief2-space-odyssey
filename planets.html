PLANETS

Mercury

Smallest and closest planet to the Sun. Rocky, heavily cratered, no atmosphere to protect it.

Moons : 0
Day length : ~59 Earth days

Earth

Our home, the only known planet with liquid water and life.

Moons : 1 (the Moon)
Day length : 24 hours

Venus

Similar in size to Earth but with a thick atmosphere of CO₂ causing a runaway greenhouse effect.

Moons : 0
Day length : 243 Earth days

Nepton

The farthest planet from the Sun. An ice giant with the strongest winds in the solar system.

Moons : 14
Day length : 16 hours

Jupiter

The largest planet. A gas giant with a massive storm known as the Great Red Spot.

Moons : 95+
Day length : 10 hours

Mars

Known as the “Red Planet” due to iron oxide dust. Has the largest volcano (Olympus Mons).

Moons : 2 (Phobos, Deimos)
Day length : 24.6 hours (a little longer than Earth)
